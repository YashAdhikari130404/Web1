Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Nestlé MILKMAID
  • Flour
  • Grated Paneer
  • Baking Powder
  • Baking Soda
  • Oil
  • Water
  • Sugar
  • Crushed Cardamom

Recipe

  1. Prepare the dough by mixing milk powder, flour,
    ghee, and milk.
  2. Shape the dough into small, crack-free balls.
  3. Fry the dough balls until golden brown.
  4. Prepare a sugar syrup by boiling sugar and water.
  5. Soak the fried balls in the sugar syrup until they
    absorb the sweetness.

Kaju Katli

Ingredients

  • Cashew Nuts
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Ghee
  • Cardomom Powder

Recipe

  1. Prepare the dough and shape it into small balls.
  2. Deep-fry the dough balls until they are golden
    brown.
  3. Prepare a sugar syrup.
  4. Soak the fried dough balls in the sugar syrup.
  5. Allow the Gulab Jamun to absorb the syrup and
    serve.
